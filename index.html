ABOUT ME

I'm Andrew Liera, a Brooklyn-based UX Designer (and Developer!) by way of Kansas City, MO. My forray into the world of UX Design began at the end of 2020 where I was a student through Columbia Engineering Bootcamps, but I'd consider myself as always being tech-adjacent.
As an undergrad chemical engineering major, I was introduced to the concept of Design Thinking and given the opportunity to explore a few different areas in tech, primarily edutech, and always wondered where I would be able to take that knowledge professionally.
Some years later and I am now currently trying to find opportunities to break into the field that I think I'd be able to do the most good, not just for myself, but for the people who might need my help.
You can find me at my laptop, where I'm currently working on trying to improve my front-end dev skills and daydreaming about video games and narrative design.
Glad we finally got to meet! :)
